About QuizMaster AI

QuizMaster AI is a cutting-edge Chrome extension designed to help students solve quizzes and practice questions efficiently. Using advanced AI technology and vision models, it can scan your screen, extract questions, and provide accurate answers with detailed explanations.

Whether you're preparing for exams, completing assignments, or practicing MCQs, QuizMaster AI streamlines your study process. The extension features an intelligent OCR system that recognizes both multiple choice questions and regular questions, solving them instantly with high accuracy.

Our AI chat assistant provides additional support, answering your study-related questions in real-time. With QuizMaster AI, learning becomes more efficient, allowing you to understand concepts better and prepare more effectively for your academic goals.
