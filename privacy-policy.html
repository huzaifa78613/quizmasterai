Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

QuizMaster AI ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension for quiz solving and AI assistance.

Information We Collect

QuizMaster AI processes the following information to provide its services:

  • Screenshot Data: When you use the "Scan & Solve" feature, we capture a screenshot of your active browser tab to extract and solve questions
  • Chat Messages: Text you enter in the "Ask AI" chat interface
  • Chat History: Previous conversations stored locally in your browser

How We Use Your Information

The information we process is used solely for:

  • Processing screenshots through AI vision models to extract questions
  • Generating answers and explanations using AI language models
  • Providing chat-based AI assistance for study-related queries
  • Maintaining chat history for conversation continuity

Third-Party Services

QuizMaster AI uses the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

  • Groq API: AI processing service for vision OCR and text generation
  • GitHub: Hosts remote configuration for API management

When you use QuizMaster AI:

  • Screenshots and text are sent to Groq API for AI processing
  • Groq processes this data according to their own privacy policy
  • We fetch configuration data from GitHub repositories

We recommend reviewing Groq's privacy policy at their official website to understand how they handle data.

Local Storage

QuizMaster AI stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Chat conversation history
  • Remote configuration data (API endpoints and models)

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You have complete control over this locally stored data.

Data Retention

Screenshot data and chat messages are sent to Groq API for processing but are not permanently stored by us. Groq may retain data according to their own policies. Chat history is stored locally on your browser until you clear it or uninstall the extension.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are managed remotely and never exposed to users
  • Local data is stored using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms
  • No sensitive personal information is collected or stored

What We Do NOT Collect

QuizMaster AI does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Browsing history beyond active tab screenshots
  • Credit card or payment information
  • Location data
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies for tracking purposes

User Control and Data Deletion

You can control your data at any time:

  • Chat history is stored locally and can be cleared by uninstalling the extension
  • You can clear browser data to remove all locally stored information
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all local data immediately
  • You choose when to use the screenshot feature

Children's Privacy

While QuizMaster AI is designed to help students, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The extension processes only the specific screenshots and text that users choose to submit for quiz solving purposes.

Academic Integrity

QuizMaster AI is intended as a study aid and learning tool. Users are responsible for ensuring their use of the extension complies with their institution's academic integrity policies. We encourage using the extension for practice, review, and learning rather than for completing graded assessments dishonestly.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of QuizMaster AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data is being processed when you use the extension
  • Control when screenshots are captured and text is sent to AI
  • Delete locally stored chat history at any time
  • Stop using the service at any time by uninstalling the extension
